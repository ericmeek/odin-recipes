Scalloped Potatoes

Description

Simple, yummy scalloped potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 6 Medium Potatoes
  • 2 Teaspoons salt
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup shredded swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degress F
  2. Arrange sliced potatoes in casserole dish
  3. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat
  4. Mix all ingredients
  5. Serve
